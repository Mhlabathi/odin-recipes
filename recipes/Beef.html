Beef

A hearty beef stew made with love

From flavours of the fresh garden to the taste of a hearty warm tender meat. This beef stew is a representation of a sunday meal that will make a family gathering the best part. great for all occassions as well.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Pepper
  • Carrot
  • Potatoes
  • Garlic
Steps
  1. Prepare onions, pepper and garlic
  2. Put oil in the pot, make sure it just to greace the base of the pot
  3. Put the onions in the warming oils
  4. Leave for 2 mins, thereafter add garlic and papper
  5. Let it simmer for another 3-5 minutes
