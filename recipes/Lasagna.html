Lasagna

Home made lasagna for a treat

Lets take a street like taste with a catchy flavour of lemon on this dish of classic lasagna. Made to surprise your taste buds but definatley tentalise them parallely unmatched.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Cottage cheese
  • Carrots
  • Pepper
  • Ground beef
  • Pastry
  • Half a lemon
  • Chicken Breasts/Fillets
  • Mayonnaise
Steps
  1. Prepare onions, pepper
  2. Great your carrots and juice with the slice of lemon
  3. Cut you chicken fillet/breasts into small pieces(To your prefarred serving size)
  4. Mix the chicken pieces with the ground beef and add a bit of salt, Mayonnaise
